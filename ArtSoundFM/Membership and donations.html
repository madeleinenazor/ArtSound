Membership

If you love what we do, consider becoming a member of ArtSoundFM. Your membership entitles you to some great benefits – but most importantly, you’ll have the satisfaction of supporting Canberra arts and music.

  • Individual Supporter

    $70/Year

    As an Individual Supporter, you not only support local music and radio but also get exclusive content, prizes and music.

  • Consession Supporter

    $40/Year

    Get the same perks but at a discount. Available to NewStart, Youth Allowance, Pension and Centrelink card holders.

  • Family Supporter

    $100/Year

    Sign up your whole family or a bunch of friends at a discounted rate. Exclusive content prizes and music available.

  • Passionate Supporter

    $70/Year + $100 Donation

    Why not become a Passionate Supporter and donate while you become a member. All donations are tax deductable.
