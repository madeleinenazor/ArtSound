Membership

If you love what we do, consider becoming a member of ArtSoundFM. Your membership entitles you to some great benefits – but most importantly, you’ll have the satisfaction of supporting Canberra arts and music.

  • Individual Supporter

    Oct 31st - Nov 6th

    All Day

    Thank you to everyone who has already renewed, joined or donated. You have already been entered into our Member’s Week prize draws.This year, thanks to our donors and business partners, we have some wonderful prizes every day from 31 October to 6 November.

  • Family Supporter

    Oct 31st

    10:30am

    From traditional and contemporary grooves to the quirky and the endearing; join us for a repeat of Saturday night’s program. Soak up the atmosphere featuring concerts recorded by ArtSoundFM at the iconic National Folk Festival, held each Easter at Exhibition Park in Canberra.

  • Passionate Supporter

    Nov 1st

    11:00am

    Everyone has a favourite showtune. Sometimes we don’t even know a favourite song was originally a showtune! The Broadway Musicals Yearbook is full of surprises like that. Each week, he’ll play songs from Broadway musicals that opened in a particular year – the hit shows and the flops.
